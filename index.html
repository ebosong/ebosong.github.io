Research Interests

Embodied AI

Foundations of intelligent agents that perceive, reason, and act through continuous interaction with physical or simulated worlds.

Aerial Embodied Intelligence

Decision and control for UAV systems under long-range navigation, uncertainty, and dynamic scene constraints.

Robotics & Manipulation

Learning-driven manipulation and robot autonomy with robust perception-action coupling in unstructured settings.

Autonomous Driving

Closed-loop planning and policy learning for safe and adaptive driving behavior in complex traffic environments.

Vision-Language-Action

VLA architectures that align semantic understanding with grounded actions for embodied agents.

Reinforcement Learning

Sample-efficient RL algorithms for long-horizon control, exploration, and policy generalization across tasks.

World Models

Latent predictive modeling for planning, simulation, and uncertainty-aware decision making in embodied systems.

Multimodal Decision Making

Joint modeling of visual, linguistic, spatial, and temporal cues for robust downstream control and planning.
